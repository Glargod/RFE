RFE

Robert Field Effect

A conceptual framework: apparent static equilibria are continuous, non-dissipative energy exchanges and configurational maintenance across scales.

Core Postulates

  • Continuum of energetic negotiation: No true rest state exists; every equilibrium is ongoing balance between cohesive and dispersive forces.
  • Constraint-induced deformation as activity: Compatible media (steel for magnets, bonds for atoms) enforce non-native geometry — persistent maintenance without macroscopic work.
  • Work redefined broadly: Classical F·dr is only one form; sustained balances, field coercion, Pauli enforcement, potential resistance all qualify as configurational "work".
  • Material-dependent activation: Effect activates with partners; incompatible ones (wood, vacuum) leave relaxed states where external potentials dominate.

Key Insight

Static stability is not the absence of process, but the most efficient, lossless mode of perpetual energetic interaction — a kind of "zero-dissipation work".

Potential energy is antijoules: energy invested to prevent relaxation. Equilibrium demands continuous (invisible) effort. There is no free energy — only pre-paid potential waiting behind barriers, extractable only at cost.

Origins & Scope

Born from magnets defying gravity on steel (vs. falling near wood), extended to atoms, nuclei, cosmic structures. Contradicts "equilibrium = nothing happening" to reveal hidden maintenance.

Scale matters: strongest where barriers are highest and decay slowest — from quantum zero-point to gravitational wells.
